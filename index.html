Discover Henry W. Coe State Park

This State Park is located in the hills near Pacheco Pass in the Coast Range. It is treated by Silicon Valleyites as part of their recreational back yard. People of the San Joaquin Valley should consider this park to be in their back yard as well. It's a gem!
